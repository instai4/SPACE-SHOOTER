# Stellar Defense 🚀

An enhanced space shooter arcade game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. Defend your ship against waves of enemies, collect power-ups, and achieve the highest score!

## 🎮 Play Online

You can play the game directly in your browser: [Stellar Defense Demo](#) 

## 📖 Description

Stellar Defense is a fast-paced space shooter where you control a starship and must survive increasingly difficult waves of enemy ships. The game features:

- Smooth dual-joystick controls on mobile (left stick to move, right stick to aim)
- Desktop controls with keyboard and mouse
- Combo system for score multipliers
- Power-ups (health, shield, special weapon)
- Boss battles every 5 waves
- Particle effects and dynamic starfield
- Responsive design for both desktop and mobile devices

## ✨ Features

- **Dual Joystick Mobile Controls**: Move with left joystick, aim independently with right joystick.
- **Desktop Support**: WASD/arrow keys to move, mouse to aim, click or space to fire.
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Enemies get stronger each wave.
- **Combo Multiplier**: Chain kills to multiply your score.
- **Power-ups**:
  - ❤️ Health: Restores 30% hull.
  - 🛡️ Shield: Full shield recharge.
  - ⭐ Special Weapon: Temporary spread shot.
- **Visual Effects**: Explosions, muzzle flashes, screen shake on damage.
- **Pause / Resume**: Press `P` to pause.
- **Game Over Screen**: Shows final score, kills, waves survived, and max combo.
- **Responsive Layout**: Works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

## 🎯 How to Play

Your objective is to survive as many waves as possible while destroying enemy ships. Each enemy destroyed adds to your score. Build up your combo by killing enemies quickly without taking damage.

- **Enemy ships** come in three types: normal, heavy (more health), and bosses (every 5 waves).
- **Collect power-ups** that fall from destroyed enemies.
- **Avoid enemy bullets** and collisions.
- The game ends when your hull reaches zero.

| Action          | Control                |
|-----------------|------------------------|
